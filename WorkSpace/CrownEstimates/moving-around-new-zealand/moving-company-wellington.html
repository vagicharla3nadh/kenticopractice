Wellington Moving Company

Wellington may be New Zealand’s capital city, but that doesn’t mean it is resting on its laurels. It’s also home to Parliament, the Museum of New Zealand/Te Papa, a thriving film industry, and scores of funky cafes, shops, and galleries.

This cosmopolitan city isn’t short on ancient history either – the earliest Maori name for Wellington is 'Te Upoko o te Ika a Maui' (the head of the fish of Maui), stemming from the Maori legend of how Polynesian navigator Maui fished up the North Island. You can find out more about Wellington’s early Maori and European history on Te Ara.

Here at Crown Relocations we specialise in the packing, storage, and transportation of household goods both within New Zealand and internationally. Because of our years of experience as a moving company in Wellington, the Crown Wellington branch is a key part of what makes Crown Relocations a worldwide leader in the Relocations Industry. Our great central location, and Wellington’s thriving community of business and political leaders, means we not only move our clients around Wellington, New Zealand, and the world, but we regularly manage inbound relocations as more and more people figure out 'you can’t beat Wellington on a good day'.
